Popular

Heat Pump Training

3–5 Days Accredited

Learn to install and commission high-quality, high-efficiency air source and ground source heat pump systems. This comprehensive course covers system design, installation best practices and MCS requirements.

Who is this for? Experienced plumbers, heating engineers and gas installers looking to diversify into renewable heating technologies.

What you'll learn:

  • Heat pump system design and sizing
  • Installation and commissioning procedures
  • Refrigerant handling and safety
  • MCS standards and compliance
  • Fault finding and maintenance

Solar PV Installation

3 Days Accredited

Design, install and commission rooftop solar photovoltaic systems for domestic and commercial properties. This hands-on course covers everything from site assessment to grid connection.

Who is this for? Experienced domestic and commercial electrical operatives who want to install and maintain solar panel systems.

What you'll learn:

  • Solar PV system design and specification
  • Roof assessment and mounting systems
  • Electrical installation and wiring
  • Testing, commissioning and handover
  • MCS certification requirements

Battery Storage

2 Days Accredited

Specify and install efficiency-boosting battery storage systems alongside solar PV installations. Help your customers generate, store and use their own power while accessing the most attractive Smart Export Tariffs.

Who is this for? Experienced domestic and commercial operatives, particularly those already working with solar PV installations.

What you'll learn:

  • Battery storage system types and specification
  • Installation and integration with solar PV
  • Smart export tariffs and energy management
  • Safety considerations and regulations
  • Commissioning and customer handover

EV Charging Installation

1–2 Days Accredited

Learn to specify and install electric vehicle charge points for domestic and commercial properties. Take advantage of the rapidly growing EV market with in-demand installation skills.

Who is this for? Experienced electricians and electrical engineers wanting to upskill and enter the EV charging installation market.

What you'll learn:

  • EV charge point types and specifications
  • Site assessment and supply requirements
  • Installation, wiring and earthing
  • Smart charging and load management
  • Testing, commissioning and certification
