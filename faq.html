Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our training courses, accreditations and enrolment process.

Entry requirements vary by course. Most of our courses require relevant trade experience — for example, an electrical, plumbing, or heating engineering background. Specific prerequisites are listed on each course description.

If you're unsure whether you meet the entry requirements, get in touch and our team will be happy to advise you on the best training path.

Course duration varies depending on the qualification:

  • EV Charging Installation: 1–2 days
  • Battery Storage: 2 days
  • Solar PV Installation: 3 days
  • Heat Pump Training: 3–5 days

All courses include both theoretical and practical hands-on training.

RETA is a NICEIC accredited training centre. Our courses lead to industry-recognised qualifications that support your applications to competent person schemes and MCS (Microgeneration Certification Scheme) certification.

For full details, visit our Accreditations page.

Our training centre is located in Mold, Flintshire, North Wales. We're easily accessible for trainees from across Wales and the North West of England, with good road links via the A55 and A494.

Visit our Contact page for directions and a map.

Funding availability changes regularly depending on government schemes and regional initiatives. We work to make training as accessible as possible and will always let you know about any current funding options.

Contact us to discuss current funding options that may be available for your chosen course.

Booking is simple:

  1. Browse our courses page to find the right training for you
  2. Submit an enquiry via our enquiry form
  3. Our team will get back to you to discuss availability, prerequisites and confirm your booking

Alternatively, call us on 01234 567 890 or email info@reta-training.co.uk.

Upon successful completion of your course, you will receive an industry-recognised qualification certificate. This can be used to support applications to competent person schemes, MCS registration, and demonstrate your competency to clients and employers.

Yes, free parking is available for all trainees at our Mold training centre. Details of parking arrangements will be provided in your booking confirmation.

  • Photo ID (driving licence or passport)
