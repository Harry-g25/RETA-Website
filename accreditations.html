NICEIC Accredited Training Centre

RETA holds full accreditation from NICEIC (National Inspection Council for Electrical Installation Contracting), one of the UK's leading voluntary regulatory bodies for the electrical contracting industry.

Our NICEIC accreditation means that our training facilities, course content and instructors have been assessed and approved to meet rigorous industry standards. This gives you the confidence that your training is delivered to the highest quality and that your qualifications will be fully recognised by employers, contractors and regulatory bodies.

What NICEIC accreditation means for you:

  • Qualifications recognised across the industry
  • Training delivered to independently verified standards
  • Support for competent person scheme applications
  • Credibility with clients, employers and regulatory bodies
  • Confidence that your training meets current regulations

What Accredited Training Means for You

Choosing an accredited training provider ensures your investment in training delivers real, lasting value.
